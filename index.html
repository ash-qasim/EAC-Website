Let's Talk About Personal Branding!

  • December 7th, 2021
  • Abdilatif Sheik-Qasim

Whether you know it or not everyone has a personal brand. Think of it as an aesthetic, the things you like the things you do and the things you represent. It's the way you present yourself to society, it's essentially yourself identity. But why is it so important?

Well in this day and age of technological advancement it's common for recruiters to check out your social media, and no saying "I don't have any social media..." isn't going to work, especially if your username is MollyJefferson123. According to a 2018 CareerBuilder survey , 70% of employers use social media to find and screen canditates during the hiring process. Personal branding also allows users represent their company in a good light, if you're an employee and you go to a conference and you network and build your personal brand, your employer would be ecstatic since this is good business.

Now let's talk about that dreaded word we always hear about, networking. Networking is extremely important for getting a job in Canada, or virtually everywhere. The job market is dilluted with top students from amazing schools with amazing grades, it's like a sea of perfection with no personality. Networking allows you to stand out from the crowd and make a name for yourself, and show your personality as well as display that you're not just a GPA or a name on a resume or candidate #438634 for a job.

So remember, it's not what you know, it's who you know.
